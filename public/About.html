About Onolo Solutions

Onolo Solutions (PTY) LTD is a 100% black female owned business located in Johannesburg. A level 1 BEE company founded by Funeka Mhlongo

A solutions based company,Onolo Solutions is aimed at providing only quality,timeous solutions for your various needs,be it in the work space or for your own personal services requirements

We pride ourselves in our attention to detail. We tailor our services to best fit the clients needs and requests.

Funeka Mhlongo
Founder of onolo solutions
